the painter

tell me, painter, what shade am i today?

will you color me yellow, bright as the day?

like morning light through my window pane,

soft and warm after days of rain.


will you paint me pink when you make me blush,

when my heart beats loud in a sudden rush?

or blue on the nights when you are away,

when silence grows where your laughter lay?


will you stain me red with all this desire,

with the warmth in my chest that burns like fire?

or green with the hope that starts to bloom,

a garden alive in every room?


tell me, painter, what else can i be?

what colors still wait underneath me?


because i'll take them all, every shade you spill,

the joy, the ache, the quiet thrill.


so go on, painter, don't be still

my skin is your canvas,

paint your fill.
