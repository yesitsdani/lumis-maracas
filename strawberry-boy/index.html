strawberry boy

you came to me with strawberries in your hands

all smiles and red cheeks from the sun

you looked so happy, almost childlike

like the world had shrunk

into something sweet enough to hold


you kept talking about how you picked them

which ones looked the ripest

which ones almost slipped from your fingers

and i couldn't help but look at you

more than the berries themselves


because somehow

the strawberries looked less red

next to the blush in your face

less sweet

next to the sound of your laugh


i've always loved strawberries

their softness

their sweetness

the way they leave their taste behind


but i think i understand why now


it's because they remind me of you


something bright

something soft

something i keep wanting more of


and maybe that's why i can't stop staring

when you're all jumpy and happy

showing me what you've found


because i came for the strawberries

but somehow

i left thinking only of you
